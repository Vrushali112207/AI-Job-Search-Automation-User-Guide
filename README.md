# AI-Job-Search-Automation-User-Guide
This user guide describes how we can search a job automatically using AI
## Overview
This tool automates job searching by parsing your resume, scoring it against job requirements, scraping matching roles from LinkedIn and Naukri, and delivering a filtered list straight to your inbox — so you don't have to manually browse job boards.

## What it does for you
- Scores your resume for ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compatibility
- Searches LinkedIn and Naukri for roles matching your target title and location
- Removes duplicate and expired listings
- Sends a clean digest of qualifying jobs to your email

## Before you start 
   You will need :
- Your resume as a **PDF file**
- A target **job title** (e.g., "AI Technical Writer")
- A preferred **location**
- A valid **email address** to receive results

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Open the Job Search Tool
Go to the tool's web page.

### 2. Upload Your Resume
- Click the upload button to upload Resume 
- Only PDF format is supported.

### 3. Enter Your Job Search Details
Fill in:
      Field                Description 
      -----------------------------------------------
      Job Title        The role you're targeting
      -----------------------------------------------
      Location         City or region for job search
      -----------------------------------------------
      Email           Where your results will be sent
      -----------------------------------------------
      
### 4. Submit
Click **Submit**. Your resume is sent securely to the backend for processing.

### 5. What Happens Next (Behind the Scenes)

You don't need to do anything here — but for transparency:

 1. Your resume is parsed and scored for ATS compatibility.
 2. If your resume qualifies, the system searches LinkedIn and Naukri in parallel for matching jobs.
 3. Results are filtered to remove duplicates and expired listings.
 4. Rejected or expired listings are logged separately (not shown to you, but tracked for quality).
